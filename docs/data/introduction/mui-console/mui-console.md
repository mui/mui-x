# MUI Console

<p class="description">Use the MUI Console to manage your MUI account, licenses, recipes, and AI assistance services.</p>

## What is MUI Console?

The [MUI Console](https://console.mui.com) is the central workspace for managing everything tied to your MUI account.
Use it to handle operational tasks such as:

- Managing commercial licenses, renewals, upgrades, and service add-ons.
- Reviewing license status and account details.
- Accessing MUI Recipes and managing usage for AI-powered workflows.
- Creating, viewing, and rotating API keys for AI assistance services, including the [Data Grid AI Assistant](/x/react-data-grid/ai-assistant/).

The Console keeps these account and service management tasks in one place, so teams do not need to rely on separate support requests for routine access, billing, and key management.

## Account setup

To create an account:

1. Go to the [MUI Console](https://console.mui.com).
2. Select **Sign up**.
3. Create your account with an email address, Google, or GitHub.
4. Verify your email address if prompted.
5. Sign in to the Console and open the product or service you want to manage.

If your organization already owns MUI licenses or service subscriptions, use the email address associated with that purchase or invitation when possible.
